Mobile Design

You may hear or see the use of the phrase mobile design or responsive design when creating a website or application. But what does that mean? Research has shown that, as of 2014, we have seen the amount of time people spend on mobile devices surpass the time spent on desktop devices. Simply put, more people spend time on mobile devices. But why is that important to you? When people learn about your organization, you want them to find you and and find out how they can serve their needs. Your website should be simple to access from a mobile device and easy to use once they arrive. It should welcome them in the best way possible. Mobile design matters for that initial connection between you and your potential clients. We can help you ensure that when clients come to you, they are greeted the way you want them to be greeted.
